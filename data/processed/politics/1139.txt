Obtaining benefits through the Department of Veterans Affairs has been disproportionately more difficult for Black Americans for decades, a federal lawsuit filed Monda  alleges  “The results of VA’s racial discrimination has been to deny countless meritorious applications by Black veterans, depriving them and their families of care and support that their faithful service has earned,” the lawsuit reads  Filed in federal court by Yale Law School’s Veterans Legal Services Clinic on behalf of Conley Monk Jr
